Packing well makes your journey lighter in every sense. Here is a concise, pilgrim-tested checklist of the essentials to bring for Umrah.

## Documents and money

- Passport, visa copies and travel tickets
- A few passport-size photographs
- A small amount of local currency and a travel card

## Clothing

- Two sets of Ihram for men; modest, comfortable clothing for women
- A lightweight prayer mat and a small bag for shoes
- Comfortable, easy-to-remove sandals

## Health and comfort

- Personal medicines and a basic first-aid kit
- Unscented toiletries (scented items are restricted in Ihram)
- A refillable water bottle and some light snacks

## Helpful extras

A small notebook for supplications, a portable charger, and a money belt or neck pouch for valuables. Keep your essentials in a small daypack you can carry everywhere.

> The goal is to carry little and worship much. When in doubt, leave it out.
