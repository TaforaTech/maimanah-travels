Performing Umrah for the first time is a deeply moving experience. With a little preparation, you can focus entirely on worship rather than logistics. Here is a clear, step-by-step overview.

## Before you travel

Make your intention sincere and learn the rites in advance. Attend an orientation session, keep a small book of supplications, and settle any outstanding obligations before you leave.

## Entering Ihram

Enter the state of Ihram at the designated Miqat with the intention for Umrah. Recite the Talbiyah and observe the restrictions of Ihram with care and presence of heart.

## Tawaf and Sa'i

- Perform seven circuits of Tawaf around the Kaaba, beginning at the Black Stone.
- Pray two units behind Maqam Ibrahim if possible.
- Walk seven times between Safa and Marwah for Sa'i.

## Completing Umrah

Conclude with Halq or Taqsir — shaving or trimming the hair — which marks the completion of Umrah. Take time afterwards to pray and reflect, and make the most of every moment in the blessed precincts.

> Travel light, stay patient, and let your heart lead the journey. Our team is with you at every step.
