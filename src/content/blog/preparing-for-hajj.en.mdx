Hajj is a journey of a lifetime, and good preparation helps you perform it with calm and focus. Use this practical checklist to get ready in body, heart and logistics.

## Spiritual preparation

Begin with sincere repentance and a firm intention. Learn the rites well, increase your supplications, and seek forgiveness from those around you before you travel.

## Physical preparation

- Start walking regularly in the weeks before departure — the rites involve a great deal of walking.
- Visit your doctor, complete required vaccinations, and carry essential medicines.
- Stay hydrated and rest well to keep your energy steady.

## Practical preparation

Keep your documents organised, label your luggage clearly, and carry a small bag with essentials for the days in Mina and Arafah. Note down emergency contacts and your group details.

## During the journey

Be patient, lower your expectations of comfort, and raise your expectations of reward. Help fellow pilgrims where you can, and keep your tongue busy with remembrance of Allah.

> Preparation is part of the worship. The more ready you are, the more present your heart can be.
